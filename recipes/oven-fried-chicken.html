Oven Fried Chicken

Description

Ingredients:

  • 12 Chicken thighs
  • All-purpose flour
  • Bread crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Vegetable Oil

Directions: Dredge the chicken thighs by coating them in the flour (seasoned with salt and pepper), the eggs, and then the bread crumbs. Pour the oil into a baking dish, then add the coated chicken thighs. Sprinkle with paprika and bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes. Flip the chicken and continue baking for another 30 minutes. Drain the oven-fried chicken on paper towels before serving.
